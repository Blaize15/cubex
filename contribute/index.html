Portable

Cubes are such small in size cause of its microcontroller size. Wemos D1 mini is used as a microcontroller.

Cubes connect over the WiFi, these cubes don't hardcode your ssid and password instead it uses wifiManager mechanism.

Flexible

These Cubes are extremly flexible, thus you can connect any input/output or hub cubes according to your needs.
